World Cup 2027 Update (India)

India has started preparations for the 2027 ODI World Cup. Young players such as Shubman Gill are expected to play a major role, while experienced stars like Virat Kohli and Rohit Sharma remain central to India's plans if they maintain form and fitness. Recent discussions around the squad have highlighted a blend of youth and experience as India builds toward the tournament.

Virat Kohli & Rohit Sharma: One Last World Cup


Indian cricket stars Virat Kohli and Rohit Sharma continue to be pillars of the national team. With their vast experience, batting excellence, and record-breaking performances, both players remain important figures in India's journey toward future ICC tournaments, including the 2027 ODI World Cup.
